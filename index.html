Elevate your game with these FAQs

What are the main rules of pickleball?

Pickleball rules cover serving, scoring, faults, and net play

What equipment do I need to play pickleball?

You'll need a paddle, ball, court, and appropriate footwear

How is pickleball different from tennis?

Smaller court, lower net, slower ball, and shorter rackets

What is the court size for pickleball?

20 feet wide by 44 feet long

How do you win at pickleball?

First to 11 points with a 2-point lead, or best of 3 matches
