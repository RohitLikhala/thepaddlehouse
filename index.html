Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do I need?

You will need a pickleball paddle, balls, and appropriate court shoes.

How is pickleball different from tennis?

Pickleball uses a smaller court and paddle, a lower net, and wiffle balls.

What is the correct size for pickleball?

The court is 20 feet wide by 44 feet long.

How do you win at pickleball?

Score points by winning rallies and reaching the set point goal.
